Introduction

What is BFA, March 26, 2024

With the theme, “Asia and the World: Common Challenges, Shared Responsibilities,” the Boao Forum for Asia (BFA) Annual Conference 2024 opens on March 26 in Boao, south China's Hainan Province. This year's conference draws about 2,000 delegates from over 60 countries and regions, and more than 1,100 journalists from about 40 countries and regions.
